Project Launch and Authorship Declaration:

This NFT confirms Pavel Kruhlei’s authorship of the “Cozy” and “Family” shelter designs, their future updates, and the SafePaws Shelters concept.

Project Details:
Available shelters: “Cozy” (70×80×50 cm, 1-2 cats) and “Family” (130×95×70 cm, 4-5 cats). They feature double walls with eco-friendly filler, an L-shaped vestibule, and 6mm plywood assembly.

Mission:
Addresses animal survival challenges in cold seasons and engages families in creative, impactful work.
